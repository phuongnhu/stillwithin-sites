Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

Still Within is built on a simple principle: your financial data is nobody's business but yours. This policy explains what that means in practice.

What we collect

Nothing. Still Within does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no tracking, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party SDKs that receive your information.

Where your data lives

All your envelopes, expenses, and settings are stored in a local database on your device. They are never uploaded anywhere. The app works completely offline.

Because your data exists only on your device, it does not sync between devices. If you use Still Within on both an iPhone and an iPad, each keeps its own separate data. It also means we could not access, recover, or hand over your data even if asked, we simply never have it.

Backups

Your data is included in your normal device backups (iCloud Backup or computer backups) if you have those enabled. Those backups are managed by Apple and your device settings, not by us.

Purchases

The optional premium upgrade is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Apple's handling of that transaction is covered by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Network access

The only network communication in the app is with Apple's App Store (StoreKit) to display the premium price, process the purchase, and restore it. No other connections are made.

Data export and deletion

You can export your expenses to CSV at any time from within the app. Deleting the app deletes all of its data from your device.

Children's privacy

Still Within does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Given the app's design, we don't expect it to change in substance: there is nothing to collect.

Questions about privacy? Email support@stillwithin.app.
