Budget by purpose, not by bank

Your bank sorts spending by card and by month. But that's not how life works. A trip, a renovation, a year of clothing, a summer of dining out: each has its own timeframe and its own limit. Still Within gives each one an envelope, and expenses from different cards, bank accounts, or cash all land in the right place.

📅

Any timeframe

Two weeks, a whole year, or no dates at all. A trip envelope can open months early, because flights are paid long before you leave.

🟢

Status at a glance

Color-coded progress bars and over-budget warnings. You always know where you stand.

🌍

Foreign currencies

Track what you paid abroad and what it cost at home, side by side.

🤝

Shared expenses

Split a bill between friends. Your budget counts only your share, and settlements are tracked for you.

🧮

Fast entry

Type math right in the amount field. 12.50+8.90 just works.

Your data, portable

Export everything to CSV anytime. It's your money and your data.

Private by design

Your financial data never leaves your device. No cloud, no account, no tracking, no analytics. The app works completely offline. Read the full privacy policy.
